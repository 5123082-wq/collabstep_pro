## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM:** $50M+ annually (SBOs + agencies in the "hire a team" category)
- **Competitors:** Upwork Teams, Fiverr Studios, Toptal Teams, A.Team, Braintrust all have offerings
- **Whitespace:** PM-integrated workspace (Collabstep's advantage)

### Business Model
- **Commission:** 10% from customer (recommended)
- **Revenue potential:** $12M/year by 2027 (120M GTV √ó 10%)
- **Unit economics:** Sustainable (67% margin after ops costs)

### Customer Segments
1. **SBO (40%):** "Need a quick team, don't want to hire"
2. **ME (40%):** "Need managed team for 3-6 months"
3. **Agencies (10%):** "White-label teams to scale services"
4. **Enterprises (10%):** "Specialized teams on demand"

### MVP Strategy
- **Phase 1 (Jan‚ÄìMar):** Catalog + profiles + RFQ (discovery, low risk)
- **Phase 2 (Mar‚ÄìJun):** Deal flow + escrow + disputes (monetization)
- **Phase 3 (May+):** AI matching + verification + advanced tools

### Critical Decisions
1. ‚úÖ Commission model: **10% from customer** (clear, customer-friendly)
2. ‚úÖ Escrow requirement: **Phase 2** (not MVP, to launch faster)
3. ‚úÖ KYC level: **Soft** (warn but allow; strict in Phase 2)
4. ‚úÖ Primary segment: **SBO** (fastest adoption + largest pool)
5. ‚úÖ Workspace integration: **Separate for now** (can unify later)

### Risks & Mitigations
- **Fraud:** KYC + reputation + escrow
- **Low adoption:** Organic launch + recruitment month 3
- **Low demand:** In-app discovery + AI recommendations
- **Quality:** Rating system + dispute resolution + incentives

### Success Metrics (Q4 2026)
- 3,000 teams created
- 500 deals/month
- 95% completion rate
- Customer NPS 50+
- $4M GTV/month
