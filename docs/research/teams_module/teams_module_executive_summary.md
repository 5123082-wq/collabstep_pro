## WHAT IS THIS?

Module to enable **groups of freelancers/contractors to form teams** and offer complex services on Collabstep. Differentiator: integrated with PM workspace (projects, tasks, documents, chat).

**Example:**
- 3 developers + 1 designer form "TechStudio Team"
- Customers can "Request Quote" for a website redesign
- Deal happens entirely in Collabstep workspace (no Slack/email needed)
- Team gets 90% of payment; Collabstep gets 10% commission

## COMPETITIVE POSITIONING

### vs. Upwork Teams
- **Upwork:** Generic freelancer team tool
- **Collabstep:** Integrated workspace (projects, tasks, documents, chat)
- **Win:** Customer never leaves Collabstep

### vs. Toptal Teams
- **Toptal:** White-glove, slow (5-7 days), expensive
- **Collabstep:** Self-serve, fast (48h quote), transparent
- **Win:** Lower price, faster, full project visibility

### vs. Fiverr Studios
- **Fiverr:** Fixed-price gigs, limited depth
- **Collabstep:** Complex project management, real-time collaboration
- **Win:** For serious projects (not simple deliverables)
